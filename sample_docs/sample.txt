ACME Corporation — Remote Work Policy (2026)

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding period are eligible
for hybrid remote work. Contractors are not covered by this policy.

2. Remote days
Employees may work remotely up to three (3) days per week. At least two (2) days per
week must be worked on-site at the assigned office location. Fully remote arrangements
require written approval from a department director.

3. Equipment stipend
ACME provides a one-time home-office equipment stipend of $500 per employee. The stipend
may be used for a desk, chair, monitor, or other ergonomic equipment. Receipts must be
submitted within 30 days of purchase.

4. Core hours
Regardless of location, employees must be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to
3:00 PM in their local time zone. Meetings should be scheduled within these hours.

5. Security
Remote work must be performed over a secure, password-protected network. Public Wi-Fi may
only be used with the company VPN enabled. Company devices must have full-disk encryption
turned on.
